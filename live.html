I have been performing professionally on drums and percussion for over 20 years in a wide variety of styles and situations.

While a student at UTC I performed in student ensembles including marching band, jazz band, percussion ensemble, wind ensemble, symphonic band, and university orchestra. While a music performance major I also performed professionally with the Chattanooga Symphony and Opera.

In 2010 I moved to Birmingham, Alabama to further my music career. While in Birmingham, I worked my way up the music scene playing with some of the most in-demand musical acts in the area. I quickly made a name for myself as a substitute player due to my ability to accurately learn a large amount of material in a short amount of time.

During my time in Alabama I played as a core member or top-call sub for the following bands and artists: Trey Lewis, Matt Bennett, Sam Rife, Anna Grace Beatty, Rebel, More Cowbell, After the Crash, Spencer Maige and The Reckless, Kevin Moon, and many others!
