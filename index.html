My name is Adam Blevins and I am a professional drummer and percussionist residing in the greater Chattanooga, TN area. I teach drum and percussion lessons at several area public schools as well as in my home studio located just outside Chattanooga. In a addition to my teaching duties, I also play gigs all over the southeast as my schedule allows.


Since 1996 I have taught hundreds of students in virtually all areas of percussion and styles of music, ranging from orchestral percussion and jazz drum set to contemporary drum set styles including: pop/rock, country, Latin/Afro-Cuban, heavy metal, fusion, and more!

If you would like to book me for a live or studio gig, or if you would like to inquire about private lessons or having me work with your school drumline or percussion section, you can contact me via e-mail at blevinspercussion@gmail.com.
