My name is Adam Blevins and I am a professional drummer/percussionist residing in the greater Chattanooga Area where I teach private lessons out of my home studio, in addition to teaching percussion classes at several high and middle schools and gigging all over the southeast.

I have performed and taught students in virtually all areas of percussion and all styles of music from jazz drumset and orchestral percussion to pop/rock and country music. I have had many students attain their personal goals of passing auditions and playing in popular bands with some even pursuing careers as professional musicians and music educators.
