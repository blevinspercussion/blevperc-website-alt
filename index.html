About Me

Professional drummer and percussionist with extensive experience in performance and education. Offering private lessons and professional drumming services in the Chattanooga area.

Since 1996 I have taught hundreds of students in virtually all areas of percussion and styles of music, ranging from orchestral percussion, marching percussion and jazz drum set to contemporary drum set styles including: pop/rock, country, Latin/Afro-Cuban, heavy metal, fusion, and more.
